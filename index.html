Durham House of Pets

Durham House of Pets, founded in 2004, has more than 200 employees. In strict accordance with the formulation and quality standards of AAFCO and NRC in the United States, the company specializes in the research, production and marketing of pet food nutrition. At present, the brands of Bight Pet Food Company include "Meiziyuan", "Bight", "Elvis Presley", "C3" and so on. With the growth of Bight's brand and the accumulation of market resources, the best-selling brands are "Meiziyuan", "Bight" and so on. Brand series products involve staple grain, snacks, nutrients, wet grain and functional prescription grain.
